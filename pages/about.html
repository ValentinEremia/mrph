A BIT ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Radu

My affinity for art began when I was a child - I always loved finding forms of self expression, mainly because I was painfully shy and had trouble expressing myself in spoken word. Photography was a path I explored out of sheer curiosity but soon became engulfed in this amazing medium. What I love about it the most is how it transcends time. Its an honest look at life. That's why I strive for authenticity in my work. When I'm not working, I still love to photograph, especially my family and son. I love going camping, traveling, reading good books, an excellent cup of coffee, good conversation and anything that makes me laugh. Normally, I'm pretty laid back and kind of a jokester - I have a weird sense of humor - and trying not to take life so seriously, but I also have a strong work ethic.

Initially, photography was a place for me to experience freedom: a creative outlet, somewhere to explore and play (and, an expensive hobby.) But I soon realised that photography didn’t only grant freedom to me as the photographer behind the lens; it gifted it to the people in front of the lens, too. Sure, there’s some technical stuff going on in between the shutter and the viewfinder, but there’s mostly something magical at work when you capture people in their element. And ever since that discovery, it’s been my goal to help people feel that joy with unique, personal photography that looks and feels like them. And where better than on their wedding day?
