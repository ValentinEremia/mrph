BEHIND THE LENS

Something About Me

While I’m a wedding photographer living in Dambovita, I’m regularly asked to travel all over the Romania and abroad for destination wedding photography.

On your wedding day you’ll find me looking for those unexpected moments that make the best memories: Mum and Dad giving each other a squeeze during the vows, bridesmaids sharing some pre-ceremony drinks or your uncle hitting the dance floor.

I also make time for relaxed family photos as well as couple’s photos later in the day. I’ll swiftly capture those epic portraits at sunset and then get you back to the party. To use the adage that a picture speaks a thousand words, you can see a few of my favourite photos here and daily new images on Instagram.

CEREMONY, BANQUET

Wedding Photography

Every wedding, just like every couple, is totally unique, so I never domineer or dictate anything on the day;
Instead, i follow the rhythm of how things are naturally unfolding, and capture what happens as it happens.

The only time i’ll choreograph is group shots, if you want them, and this is only to get them over and done with as quickly as possible to give you more time for drinking and dancing!

IN LOVE, FAMILY AND CHILDREN

Love Story Photography

I am a photographer specializing in personalized photography of portraits of newborns, babies, children, family photos and maternity photos. All sessions can be done in the comfort of a professional studio or even at your home!

RETOUCHING

Product Photography

I am a product photographer and photographic retoucher with experience of studio photography, image manipulation, retouching, composition, restoration, image clipping/masking and colour correction using Adobe Photoshop CC.

Offering a skilled photographic and digital retouching service tailored exactly to your own personal needs, my aim is to provide that essential support within your workflow that prepares your image for publication online or in print media.

IN ACTION

Sports Photography
