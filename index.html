Welcome, I’m Radu.

A photographer whose aim is simple: no cheese, no clichés – just vibrant, fun, natural photos as amazing as you are.

I AM A PHOTOGRAPHER DEDICATED TO SERVING FINE PEOPLE FROM ROMANIA AND NOT ONLY, SPECIALIZING IN PORTRAITS, LIFESTYLE, COMMERCIAL AND SPORTS PHOTOGRAPHY.

BEHIND THE LENS

Something About Me

My affinity for art began when I was a child - I always loved finding forms of self expression, mainly because I was painfully shy and had trouble expressing myself in spoken word. Photography was a path I explored out of sheer curiosity but soon became engulfed in this amazing medium. What I love about it the most is how it transcends time. Its an honest look at life. That's why I strive for authenticity in my work. When I'm not working, I still love to photograph, especially my family and son. I love going camping, traveling, reading good books, an excellent cup of coffee, good conversation and anything that makes me laugh. Normally, I'm pretty laid back and kind of a jokester - I have a weird sense of humor - and trying not to take life so seriously, but I also have a strong work ethic.

CEREMONY, BANQUET

Wedding Photography

Every wedding, just like every couple, is totally unique, so I never domineer or dictate anything on the day; instead, I follow the rhythm of how things are naturally unfolding, and capture what happens as it happens. The only time I’ll choreograph is group shots, if you want them, and this is only to get them over and done with as quickly as possible to give you more time for drinking and dancing!

IN LOVE, FAMILY AND CHILDREN

Love Story Photography

I am a photographer specializing in personalized photography of portraits of newborns, babies, children, family photos and maternity photos. All sessions can be done in the comfort of a professional studio or even at your home!

RETOUCHING

Product Photography

I am a product photographer and photographic retoucher with experience of studio photography, image manipulation, retouching, composition, restoration, image clipping/masking and colour correction using Adobe Photoshop CC. Offering a skilled photographic and digital retouching service tailored exactly to your own personal needs, my aim is to provide that essential support within your workflow that prepares your image for publication online or in print media.

IN ACTION

Sports Photography

If you are looking for a photographer to get you those action shots you want, then I'm your photographer. My goal is provide those high quality action shots every athlete dreams of having of them doing the sport they love.
